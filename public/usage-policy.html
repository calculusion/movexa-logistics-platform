Usage Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Movexa is an educational logistics platform created for learning, demonstration, and portfolio purposes only. The platform does not provide real-world shipping, transportation, or courier services.

1. Project Overview

Movexa is a demonstration logistics management platform developed solely for educational, research, and portfolio purposes. The platform has been created to showcase modern web development practices, database design, authentication workflows, shipment lifecycle management, and logistics-related user experiences within a simulated environment.

Movexa enables users to explore and interact with features commonly found in logistics and supply chain management systems, including shipment creation, consignment tracking, status updates, delivery workflows, analytics dashboards, and user account management.

All data, shipment records, tracking events, delivery statuses, locations, and analytics displayed within the platform are generated for demonstration and testing purposes only. Any names, addresses, contact details, shipment identifiers, or operational metrics shown on the platform are fictional, anonymized, user-generated for testing, or otherwise intended exclusively for non-commercial use.

Movexa is not a registered logistics company, courier service, freight operator, transportation provider, or supply chain management business. The platform does not facilitate, arrange, process, transport, store, or deliver physical goods or shipments of any kind.

Users must not rely on information presented within Movexa for real-world shipping, transportation, business, legal, financial, or operational decisions.

2. Intended Use

Movexa is intended exclusively for educational, demonstration, and portfolio purposes. The platform may be used for:

  • Educational activities and academic projects
  • Software demonstrations and technical presentations
  • Portfolio showcases and skill assessments
  • Testing and simulating logistics workflows
  • Learning shipment management concepts and processes
  • Exploring shipment lifecycle tracking and status updates
  • Evaluating user interface and user experience designs
  • Demonstrating database integration and authentication workflows

Movexa must not be used for:

  • Processing or coordinating real shipments
  • Managing commercial logistics operations
  • Tracking actual freight, packages, or deliveries
  • Storing sensitive, regulated, or business-critical information
  • Making operational, financial, or transportation decisions
  • Providing logistics, courier, freight, or supply chain services

Users must not rely on Movexa for real-world logistics operations, transportation management, or commercial shipping activities.

3. Data Collection and Storage

Movexa collects and stores information necessary to provide, maintain, and improve platform functionality. Depending on how the platform is used, this information may include:

  • Account information, such as names, email addresses, and authentication details
  • Shipment records and consignment information
  • Sender and receiver details provided during shipment creation
  • User preferences and account settings
  • Technical information, including device, browser, and session data
  • Support requests, feedback submissions, and communications

Information stored within Movexa is used exclusively for educational demonstrations, feature testing, troubleshooting, analytics, security monitoring, and platform improvement.

User data is hosted and processed using secure third-party infrastructure providers that support core platform services, including authentication, database management, hosting, and system monitoring.

Users are encouraged not to submit confidential, sensitive, or business-critical information through the platform. Any shipment data entered into Movexa should be considered demonstration data only.

Movexa does not sell, rent, or trade user information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Security Measures

Movexa implements reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect user information and maintain the integrity of the platform. Security measures may include:

  • Secure user authentication and account verification processes
  • Role-based access controls and authorization mechanisms
  • Encrypted HTTPS connections for data transmitted between users and the platform
  • API rate limiting and abuse prevention measures
  • Protected database access with restricted permissions
  • Server-side input validation and sanitization
  • Session management and access token security
  • Continuous monitoring for suspicious activity and unauthorized access attempts

These measures are intended to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure of information stored within the platform.

Despite these efforts, no internet-based service, application, or data transmission method can guarantee complete security. Users acknowledge that all online platforms are subject to inherent cybersecurity risks, including service interruptions, software vulnerabilities, and unauthorized access attempts.

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials and for taking appropriate precautions when accessing the platform.

5. Prohibited Activities

To maintain a secure, reliable, and educational environment, users must not engage in activities that could disrupt, compromise, or misuse the platform.

  • Attempt to gain unauthorized access to accounts, systems, databases, APIs, or restricted areas of the platform
  • Access, modify, or retrieve information belonging to other users without permission
  • Interfere with or disrupt the normal operation, performance, or availability of the platform
  • Submit, upload, transmit, or distribute malicious code, malware, viruses, or harmful content
  • Use automated tools to disrupt the platform
  • Abuse APIs, contact forms, feedback channels, or support systems through excessive, automated, or fraudulent requests
  • Use bots, scripts, crawlers, scrapers, or other automated tools to extract data or disrupt platform functionality
  • Circumvent security controls, authentication mechanisms, rate limits, or access restrictions
  • Reverse engineer, copy, reproduce, or commercially exploit any part of the platform without authorization
  • Submit false, misleading, unlawful, defamatory, or fraudulent information
  • Create shipment records that impersonate real individuals, businesses, or organizations
  • Use the platform for any illegal, harmful, or unauthorized purpose
  • Represent, advertise, or imply that Movexa is a real logistics company, freight operator, courier service, or transportation provider

Movexa reserves the right to suspend, restrict, or terminate access to users who violate these requirements or engage in conduct that threatens the security, integrity, or intended purpose of the platform.
