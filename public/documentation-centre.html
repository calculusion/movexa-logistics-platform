Shipment Status Guide

A complete breakdown of the shipment lifecycle and what each status means.

Pending Start

The shipment has been created and is awaiting review and verification.

Accepted

The shipment has been reviewed and accepted for processing.

Started Delivery

The shipment has left the origin facility and delivery operations have started.

On Route

The shipment is currently in transit between locations.

Delayed

The shipment has encountered an unexpected delay. Delivery will resume once the issue is resolved.

Delivery Resumed

The shipment is back on schedule and continues toward its destination.

Delivered Completed

The shipment has successfully reached its destination and has been marked as completed.

Packaging Guide

Follow these best practices to ensure your items arrive safely.

Construction Materials

  • Use reinforced packaging for heavy materials
  • Secure loose components before transport
  • Protect cargo from moisture and dust

Industrial Machinery

  • Secure all moving mechanical components
  • Use heavy duty restraints during transit
  • Verify load balance before dispatch

Glass Panels

  • Apply edge guards to prevent breakage
  • Use foam cushioning for added protection
  • Clearly label shipments as fragile cargo

Electronics

  • Use anti-static packaging materials
  • Protect devices with shock absorbing cushioning
  • Pack cables and accessories separately
