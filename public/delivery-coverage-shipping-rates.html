Delivery Coverage

Movexa supports domestic and international deliveries across a growing network of cities and regions.

Domestic Coverage

Reliable shipping between supported cities within the same country.

  • Intercity freight movement
  • Standard and Express services
  • Shipment tracking

International Coverage

Cross-border delivery routes connecting supported countries across Europe.

  • Multi-country delivery network
  • Customs documentation support
  • Secure shipment handling

Priority Routes

High-demand transportation corridors optimized for faster transit.

  • Reduced transit times
  • Priority shipment processing
  • Enhanced shipment visibility

Remote Regions

Extended coverage for selected regions outside primary logistics corridors.

  • Coverage subject to destination
  • Route planning optimization
  • Additional transit time may apply
